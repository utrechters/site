# Code of Conduct

## Our Intent

This repository supports a real-world community.  
We aim to create a space that is respectful, welcoming, and constructive — both online and offline.

Participation here should feel safe and collaborative.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Give feedback on ideas, not on people.
- Be patient with contributors of all experience levels.
- Help newcomers feel welcome.

## Human Happens

Many of our community interactions also happen offline.  
People are human. Misunderstandings happen.

If something comes out wrong:
- Clarify.
- Apologize if needed.
- Move forward respectfully.

We care more about how situations are handled than about perfection.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discrimination of any kind
- Hostile or dismissive communication
- Repeated disruptive behavior

## Moderation

Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, close discussions, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Serious incidents (online or related to this project offline) may be documented privately to ensure clarity if accounts differ later. This documentation is never shared publicly and exists only for fairness and consistency.

In unclear situations, decisions may be discussed with the internal council to ensure balanced judgment.
